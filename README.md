# 📊 Employee Engagement & Satisfaction Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee survey responses to evaluate engagement, satisfaction, and overall workplace experience. The goal is to extract meaningful insights from employee feedback and support data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee engagement and satisfaction across departments
* Identify key factors affecting employee experience
* Highlight areas of improvement in workplace culture
* Provide actionable insights for HR strategies

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset consists of employee survey responses, including:

* Department, Manager, Supervisor, and Staff details
* Survey questions related to engagement and workplace experience
* Employee responses (ratings and text feedback)

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)
* **Data Visualization** (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* **Jupyter Notebook**
* **Power BI** *(to be added)*

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis

Performed analysis on key survey questions such as:

* Job satisfaction
* Recognition and feedback
* Work relationships
* Learning and growth opportunities
* Supervisor support
* Inclusion and diversity

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Employees generally understand their job expectations clearly
* Recognition and appreciation are inconsistent across teams
* Some employees lack strong workplace relationships
* Growth and learning opportunities vary across departments
* Overall job satisfaction shows mixed trends

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve employee recognition and reward systems
* Strengthen manager–employee relationships
* Enhance learning and development programs
* Promote inclusive and supportive work culture
